JUGO | Travel time optimization project


About the project (in progress)

For this project our client is JUGO. JUGO is a company which advises, implements and supports in the field of Business Intelligence. They want us to find out which are the best days (and times) to drive to a customer location with the lowest chance of hitting traffic jams. To solve this we look at datasets from KNMI (weather) and ANWB (roadwork, traffic jams). For this group project, since AI is very new to most of us, we work closely together. We split the work in two and two of us work with the dataset of "Traffic jams" and the other work on the dataset of "Roadworks".
We work with Jupyter Notebook and use libraries of Python; pandas, seaborn, NumPy, Matplotlib. You can check out our latest version of the dataset about Traffic jams.

My tasks in the project:

  • Research "What impact do various aspects of climate have on traffic?"
  • Data understanding
  • Data preparation
  • Preprocessing
  • Feature selection
  • Modeling
