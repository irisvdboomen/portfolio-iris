Webber | Saunakoning


About the project

Our client for this project was Webber. They are a company focusing on webdesign. Our assignment was to improve the Saunakoning website. The main goal for the website was to attract customers to their showroom. For this project I worked in a team of 6 people. All of our tasks (research, design and coding) were divided evenly.
Our finished website: Saunakoning.


My tasks in the project:


Research and Design:
  • Target group research
  • Interview target group
  • Wireframe of homepage, product page and contact page
  • Prototype of contact page
  • Testing of the prototype
  • Interview final product
  • Final presentation

Coding:
  • Contact page
  • One product page (sauna emmer)
  • Fix bugs of whole website

Liquid+ | Loyalties of Lucifer


About the project

Our client for this project was Team Liquid. They have a loyalty program: Liquid+. Our assignment was to create a loyalty program for a company of our own choice. Me and my group chose Lucifer Coffee Roasters, a local coffee place in Eindhoven. For this project, every group member had a role where we should focus on, I was the main front-end developer. You can check our website by clicking the button below: create an account with a random email and password, login and then you can access the website. We also created a blog for our project group: Blog UDIAC.

My tasks in the project:

Research and Design:
  • Target group research
  • Customer journeys and personas
  • Wireframe of homepage
  • Mockups rewards page and homepage
  • Testing of the prototype
  • Spellcheck documents and website

Coding (front-end):
  • Header, navigation bar and footer
  • Homepage/Rewards pages/Locations page/Profile page
  • Helped group members with issues and responsive version of their pages
