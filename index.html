Webber | Saunakoning


About the project

Our client for this project was Webber. They are a company focusing on webdesign. Our assignment was to improve the Saunakoning website. The main goal for the website was to attract customers to their showroom. For this project I worked in a team of 6 people. All of our tasks (research, design and coding) were divided evenly.
Our finished website: Saunakoning.


My role in the project:


Research and Design:
  • Target group research
  • Interview target group
  • Wireframe of homepage, product page and contact page
  • Prototype of contact page
  • Testing of the prototype
  • Interview final product
  • Final presentation

Coding:
  • Contact page
  • One product page (sauna emmer)
  • Fix bugs of whole website
